# Grant Opportunities -- SPRAYCATION 2026

Research these grants and apply where eligible. Most require 6-12 months lead time.

## National Grants

### National Endowment for the Arts (NEA)
- **Grants for Arts Projects** -- supports public art, community engagement
- **Our Town** -- creative placemaking, community development through arts
- Typical award: $10,000-$100,000
- Requires 501(c)(3) or fiscal sponsor; 1:1 cost share/match required
- FY 2027 deadlines: February 12, 2026 and July 9, 2026
- URL: arts.gov/grants

### Americans for the Arts
- **Public Art Network** -- resources for touring public art projects
- **Arts & Economic Prosperity** reports useful for grant applications
- URL: americansforthearts.org

### Pollock-Krasner Foundation
- Grants for visual artists (painters, sculptors, works on paper) with established work
- Rolling deadline -- applications accepted year-round
- Awards up to $50,000; financial need basis
- URL: pkf.org

### Joan Mitchell Foundation
- Emergency grants and career support for visual artists
- URL: joanmitchellfoundation.org

### Awesome Foundation
- $1,000 micro-grants for "awesome" projects
- Simple application, monthly deadlines
- URL: awesomefoundation.org

### Burning Man Arts Grants
- Given Zuzu's Burning Man community involvement, this is a natural fit
- Honoraria grants for art projects
- URL: burningman.org/culture/burning-man-arts/grants/

### FCA (Foundation for Contemporary Arts)
- Emergency Grants for visual artists
- URL: foundationforcontemporaryarts.org

### Forecast Public Art
- Mid-Career Project Grants ($10,000 each) for publicly accessible artwork
- Mid-Career Professional Development Grants for travel, study, skill development
- Note: Minnesota residency required for their core grants, but worth monitoring for national expansions
- URL: forecastpublicart.org

### Anonymous Was A Woman (AWAW)
- **Environmental Art Grants** -- 2026 cycle distributing over $470,000; projects must have a free public engagement component; deadline April 7, 2026
- **Annual Award** -- unrestricted $50,000 grants to 15 woman-identifying artists over 40
- URL: anonymouswasawoman.org

### Amber Grants for Women
- Monthly grants ($10,000) for women-owned businesses, including creative arts category
- URL: ambergrantsforwomen.com

### Barbara Deming Memorial Fund (Money for Women)
- $500-$1,500 grants twice yearly for feminists in visual art and writing
- URL: demingfund.org

### Women's Studio Workshop
- Residency grants and project funding for women artists
- 24-hour studio access with housing
- URL: wsworkshop.org

### Tory Burch Foundation Fellowship
- Women entrepreneurs fellowship with mentorship and funding
- URL: toryburchfoundation.org

## Fiscal Sponsorship Options
If SPRAYCATION doesn't have 501(c)(3) status, partnering with a fiscal sponsor allows accepting tax-deductible donations and applying for grants that require nonprofit status:
- **Fractured Atlas** -- arts-focused fiscal sponsorship (most common for touring artists)
- **New York Foundation for the Arts (NYFA)** -- fiscal sponsorship
- **Austin Creative Alliance** -- local option for Austin-based artists
