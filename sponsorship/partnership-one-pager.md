### About the Tour
SPRAYCATION is a six-month, multi-city public art tour led by muralist Zuzu. In 2025, five large-scale "You Are Magic" murals were painted across the western U.S. and Canada. Every stop brought community connection, local business visibility, and a powerful message: creativity transforms cities.

### The Purpose
Art that moves people. Art that inspires conversation, sparks joy, and strengthens neighborhoods. SPRAYCATION creates public art that belongs to everyone -- not in a gallery, not behind a paywall, but on the streets where communities live.

### 2025 Impact at a Glance
- 5 large-scale murals completed
- 6,500+ miles traveled
- 5 cities activated across 10 states + Canada
- 3x foot traffic increase (Denver activation)
- Featured in Westword, EASTside Magazine
- All-female creative team
