# 2024 Tour Recap (Year 1)

## Overview

- Solo debut tour by Zuzu
- 6,000+ miles across the western U.S.
- 5 months (summer 2024)
- 5 large-scale murals
- 10 states
- Vehicle: AquaBurn RV

## Significance

The 2024 tour proved the concept: one artist, one RV, and a vision to bring public art to communities across America. It validated that SPRAYCATION could:

- Scout, design, and execute large-scale murals
- Activate local communities
- Generate press and media coverage
- Create beautiful, meaningful content
- Run a cross-city creative operation

## What It Set Up

The solo 2024 tour laid the foundation for the expanded 2025 team tour and the 2026 brand partner scaling strategy.
