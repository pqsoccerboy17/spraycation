# SPRAYCATION -- Overview

## What It Is

SPRAYCATION is a female-led, nationwide traveling mural tour that transforms blank walls into colorful, community-centered public art. It combines "spray paint" and "vacation" into a multi-city creative movement.

## Mission

More than a mural tour -- a bold declaration that creativity, when ignited from within, has the power to transform people, places, and culture. Rooted in the belief that self-expression is a form of liberation. Fuses art, entrepreneurship, and soul-led storytelling into a scalable ecosystem.

## Campaign Theme

"YOU ARE MAGIC" -- a global message encouraging creative expression, connection, and empowerment.

## Core Message

> "Spraycation brings color, culture, and connection to cities, and 2026 is the moment we partner with big brands to scale that magic nationwide."

## What Each Tour Stop Includes

- Wall scouting and community partnership
- Large-scale mural creation (1-3 days on site)
- Professional photo + video documentation
- Mural reveal celebration event
- Press and media outreach
- Social media content creation

## Social Impact

- Economic growth for host businesses
- Neighborhood revitalization
- Foot traffic increase (3x proven in Denver)
- Community engagement and connection
- Cultural identity and pride
- Crime deterrence through beautification
