CASE STUDY

DENVER

Fever Dream Vintage / 2903 E. Colfax Ave

Scroll

01 -- The Location

A Beloved Wall on Historic Colfax

Fever Dream Vintage, a beloved vintage clothing store on historic Colfax Avenue, offered their exterior wall for Spraycation's signature "You Are Magic" mural. One of Denver's most vibrant corridors became the canvas for something unforgettable.

02 -- The Activation

Two Hours That Changed Everything

A 2-hour mural reveal event brought together the local community, press, and brand partners. Live painting, music, and the energy of creation turned a Saturday afternoon into a cultural moment on Colfax Avenue.

03 -- The Results

foot traffic increase during activation weekend

04 -- The Quote

"We've been here for 3 years, and this one mural made more people stop, talk, and share than any ad we've paid for." -- Owner, Fever Dream Vintage

05 -- What's Next

The Momentum Is Building

4 Denver partners already committed for 2026. What started as one wall on Colfax is becoming a movement across the Mile High City. The proof is on the wall -- and in the numbers.
