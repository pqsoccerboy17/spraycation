The Movement

Spraycation is more than a mural tour.

It's a bold declaration that creativity belongs everywhere --

on buildings, in neighborhoods, across state lines.

Led by artist Zuzu Perkal,

we transform blank walls into cultural landmarks

that spark joy, foot traffic, and community pride.

In 2025, we drove 6,500+ miles across 5 cities,

painting murals that generated press coverage,

tripled foot traffic for local businesses,

and proved that public art is

the most powerful marketing tool nobody's using.
