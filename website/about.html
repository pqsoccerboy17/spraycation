The Artist

Zuzu Perkal

I have pink hair and I paint walls.

Austin-based muralist and creative entrepreneur, Zuzu is a UT Austin graduate with 17+ years of creating public art that transforms communities. Named Austin's Best Muralist four consecutive years (2021-2024), she has collaborated with American Express, Coca-Cola, Red Bull, MLB, Rolling Stone, and more.

Zuzu created Spraycation to prove that female-led art can transform communities and create real business value. What started as a solo road trip with a spray can became a multi-city movement -- and a mission to put color on every wall that needs it.

@zuzubee ihavepinkhair.com spraythefuture@gmail.com

The Mission

More Than Murals

Spraycation is more than murals. It's proof that art creates economic value, that women can lead bold creative ventures, and that every community deserves beauty on its walls. We're building a new model -- where brands, artists, and neighborhoods all win.

The Journey

How We Got Here

2024

Solo debut. 5 murals. 6,000+ miles. 10 states. A spray can, an RV named AquaBurn, and a mission to prove that public art could do more than decorate -- it could transform. Proof of concept achieved.

2025

5 cities activated. 6,500+ miles. First international stop in Vancouver. Press coverage in Westword and EASTside Magazine. 3x business impact proven in Denver. The movement found its stride.

2026

8-10 cities nationwide. Strategic brand partnerships. National press outreach. Larger murals, bigger events, deeper community integration. The movement goes big.
